## Visual Theme
A high-contrast, structural interface built for local trust. High negative space offset by crisp borders, heavy technical layouts, and highly legible typography that feels like a modern blueprint.

## Art Direction
- Concept: Structural lakeshore construction system.
- Layout rhythm: Dense, high-impact block structure with contrasting light/dark sections.

## Typography
- Scale: Standard commercial
- h1: clamp(2.5rem, 5vw, 4rem)
- h2: clamp(1.8rem, 4vw, 2.75rem)
- Headlines: 'Plus Jakarta Sans', sans-serif - Bold, clean-cut, technical look
- Body: 'Inter', sans-serif - Highly legible, comfortable reading tracking

## Components
- Buttons: Sharp rectangular buttons with very subtle rounding (rounded-sm), transition-colors.
- Cards: Solid borders (border border-border) instead of drop shadows, white background surfaces.
- Sections: Alternating between high-contrast dark sections (blue accents) and clean white workspace grids.

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO use clean, high-contrast layout grids that segment services clearly.
- DO provide immediate tap-to-call links with both regional office phone numbers.
- DON'T use fancy abstract shapes, organic curves, or low-contrast text on bright backgrounds.
- DON'T use decorative icons; use clean, functional indicators for technical categories.
